This project investigates how new digital technologies can support the musical practice of Brazilian choro.
Our research combines musicological expertise and cultural heritage research 
with computational methods for large-scale music analysis 
and the development of AI-driven tools for harmonic analysis and automatic harmonization of choro melodies.

We are based at the [Zentrum für Philologie und Digitalität](https://www.uni-wuerzburg.de/zpd/) (ZPD) at [Julius-Maximilians-Universität Würzburg](https://www.uni-wuerzburg.de) 
and collaborate closely with the [Instituto Casa do Choro](https://acervo.casadochoro.com.br/) in Rio de Janeiro, home to the world's largest choro archive.

Read more on our [About](about) page.
