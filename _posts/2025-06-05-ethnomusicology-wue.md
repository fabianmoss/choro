On June 5th, 2025, Dr. Orsini Windholz presented in the Ethnomusicology Research seminar, led by Prof. Juniper Hill, at the University of Würzburg. The talk, titled Computational Analysis of Brazilian Choro, discussed the potential of deploying computational methods to study musical data, and dived deeper in the specific case of choro, proposing avenues for future investigation.
