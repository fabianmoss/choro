We are more than happy to announce that the Funding Programme for 
the Initiation of International Research Cooperation in Bavaria (BayIntAn)
of the Bavarian Resarch Alliance (BayFOR)
granted us funding for a research trip to Rio de Janeiro, including 
a visit to the score archive of Casa do Choro and scientific exchange at 
Universidade Federal do Rio de Janeiro!
