On January 28, 2026, Dr. Orsini Windholz gave a second talk at the Ethnomusicology Research Seminar, led by Prof. Juniper Hill, in Würzburg. The talk presented the results of Windholz's first research project on choro harmony, which carried out a data-driven comparison of the style with the harmonies of classical, jazz and Anglophone popular music.
